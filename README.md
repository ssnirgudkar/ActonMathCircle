This is a personal project of teaching elementary and middle school kids how to "do" math.
I believe if kids discover the solutions themselves, they will remember it. So the focus is not on giving instructions but gently nudging them to 
come up with their hypothesis, creative ideas. Philosphy of Math Circle is perfect for such an endevour. 
By using games and puzzles which have deep underlying mathematical structure, the kids get exposed to variety of math topics usually not covered in school
curriculum.
