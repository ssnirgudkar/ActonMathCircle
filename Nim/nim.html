Nim-sum (XOR) —

The Nim-sum is the XOR (bitwise exclusive-or) of all pile sizes. A position is a losing position for the player about to move if and only if the Nim-sum equals 0.

Things to Discover

Conjecture freely
① Equal piles always lose
Set both piles to the same size — try (2,2), (3,3), (4,4). Play a few rounds. What do you notice about who wins? Can you explain why equal piles are bad for the first player?
② Add a third pile
Increase to 3 piles. Try (1,2,3) — is this a win or loss for Player 1? What about (1,3,5)? What about (2,3,4)? Build a small table and look for a pattern in which positions lose.
③ The Misère flip
Toggle Misère mode (last to take loses). Does every position that was a win become a loss? Or is it more subtle? Try the same starting positions in both modes and compare.
